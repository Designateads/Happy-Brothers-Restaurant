    Get to Know more About us

    Happy Brothers Bar and Grill is a premier dining establishment that specializes in providing exceptional food and beverage services. our Resturant is dedicated to delivering high-quality, delectable meals that cater to a variety of events, functions, meetings, and any occasion requiring outstanding catering services.

    • Event Catering: Full-service catering for weddings, parties, corporate events and more
    • Function Catering: Customized menus for functions, ensuring a delightful culinary experience.
    • Meeting Catering: Convenient and delicious meal options for business meetings and conferences
    • Special Occasion Catering: Unique and personalized catering for birthdays, anniversaries and other celebrations

    Our mission is to create memorable dining experiences by offering a diverse menu of mouth-watering dishes prepared with the finest ingredients. We aim to provide exceptional service and ensure customer satisfaction at every event we cater.

    Double the Flavor, Double the Delight!

    Best Braai

    The Chicken Worse Steak Combo at Happy Brother's Bar and Grill is a culinary delight designed to satisfy the most discerning palates. This enticing meal features a succulent, perfectly grilled chicken breast paired with a juicy, tender steak, seasoned to perfection. The combo is complemented by a side of garlic mashed potatoes, a fresh garden salad, and a choice of savory sauces that enhance the rich flavors of both meats. Whether you’re in the mood for the hearty taste of steak or the lighter, flavorful chicken, this combo offers the best of both worlds, making it an ideal choice for any meat lover. Join us at Happy Brother's Bar and Grill to experience this mouthwatering feast!

    Experience the best of our Kotas

    we call it the Jumbo

    Experience the ultimate South African street food with our Jumbo Kota at Happy Brothers Bar and Grill. This hearty dish features a quarter loaf of freshly baked bread, stuffed with your choice of juicy beef, tender chicken, or flavorful boerewors. Topped with crispy fries, fresh lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and a tangy, creamy sauce, the Jumbo Kota is a delicious blend of flavors and textures. Perfect for a satisfying meal, this iconic dish is a must-try for all food lovers
